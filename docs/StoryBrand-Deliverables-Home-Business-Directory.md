## Executive Summary

This document provides a complete StoryBrand marketing framework for a free home-based business directory. The strategy positions home-based business owners as heroes on a journey to find customers, with the directory serving as their trusted guide. All messaging follows Donald Miller's proven 7-part StoryBrand framework to create clear, compelling marketing that converts.

## The BrandScript

The BrandScript is the foundation of all StoryBrand messaging. Every piece of marketing content should flow from these seven elements.

### 1. Character (The Hero)

**Who they are:** Home-based business owners who have something valuable to offer but struggle to get noticed.

**What they want:** More customers, consistent leads, and a steady stream of business without expensive marketing.

**Core desire:** To be found by people who need exactly what they offer‚Äîand to build a thriving business from home.

### 2. Problem

#### External Problem (The Villain)
Home-based businesses are invisible online. They're buried under big brands with big budgets, lost in crowded marketplaces, or stuck relying on word-of-mouth that doesn't scale.

#### Internal Problem
They feel frustrated, overlooked, and unsure how to compete. Many wonder if their business will ever get the attention it deserves. There's a nagging fear that they're doing something wrong because no one seems to find them.

#### Philosophical Problem
Good, honest home-based businesses shouldn't be invisible. Hardworking entrepreneurs deserve to be discovered by people who need their services. Marketing shouldn't be a barrier to success.

### 3. Guide (Your Brand)

#### Empathy Statement
> "We know how frustrating it is to pour your heart into your business and feel like no one can find you. Building something great at home shouldn't mean staying invisible."

#### Authority Credentials
- Free directory designed specifically for home-based businesses
- Optimized for local search and customer discovery
- Trusted by [X] home-based businesses (update with real numbers)
- Built to help businesses like yours get found

### 4. The Plan

**The 3-Step Plan:**

1. **Create Your Free Listing** ‚Äì Add your business name, services, and contact info in minutes.
2. **Get Found by Local Customers** ‚Äì Your listing appears when people search for services like yours.
3. **Grow Your Business** ‚Äì Connect with customers who are already looking for what you offer.

### 5. Call to Action

#### Direct CTA
- "List Your Business Free"
- "Get Listed Now"
- "Claim Your Free Listing"

#### Transitional CTA
- "Download: 5 Ways to Get More Customers From Home"
- "Free Guide: Stand Out in Your Local Market"
- "See How Other Home Businesses Get Found"

### 6. Success (The Stakes)

**Paint the picture of success:**
- Customers reaching out to you (instead of you chasing them)
- A growing business without expensive advertising
- Confidence that people can find you when they need you
- Financial stability and growth working from home
- Recognition as a legitimate, trustworthy local business

### 7. Failure (What's at Stake)

**What they avoid by taking action:**
- Staying invisible while competitors get all the customers
- Wasting money on marketing that doesn't work
- Giving up on a business dream because no one knows you exist
- Continued frustration and uncertainty about the future

## Nurture Email Sequence

Send these emails after someone downloads your lead generator:

### Email 1: Deliver the Value (Day 0)

**Subject:** Your free guide is here

**Content:** Deliver the lead generator. Brief intro reinforcing you understand their struggle. One tip from the guide. Soft mention that listing in your directory is the fastest way to get found.

### Email 2: Problem + Empathy (Day 2)

**Subject:** The #1 reason home businesses stay invisible

**Content:** Address the external problem (hard to get noticed). Empathize with the internal problem (frustrating, feels unfair). Position your directory as the solution without a hard sell.

### Email 3: Testimonial/Proof (Day 4)

**Subject:** How [Name] went from invisible to fully booked

**Content:** Share a success story of a home-based business that got results from your directory. Include specific numbers if possible. End with soft CTA to list their business.

### Email 4: Overcome Objections (Day 6)

**Subject:** "I don't have time" (and other myths)

**Content:** Address common objections: takes too long (it's 3 minutes), won't work for my industry (works for all home businesses), free means low quality (explain your model). Direct CTA to list.

### Email 5: The Stakes (Day 9)

**Subject:** What happens if you stay invisible?

**Content:** Paint the picture of failure (staying invisible, watching competitors grow, possibly giving up). Then contrast with success (customers finding you, business growing). Strong CTA.

### Email 6: Final Push (Day 12)

**Subject:** Your customers are looking for you

**Content:** Direct, action-focused email. People are searching right now for services like theirs. The directory is free. Takes 3 minutes. What are they waiting for? Strong, clear CTA.
